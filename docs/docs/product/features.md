# AutoBlog Product Features

## Core Features

### 1. Automated Content Discovery & Strategy

#### Daily Trend Discovery
- **Web Search Intelligence**: Real-time discovery of trending topics using OpenAI's web search capabilities
- **Industry-Specific Filtering**: Content opportunities tailored to your brand's domain and audience
- **Trend Scoring Algorithm**: AI-powered relevance scoring based on search volume, competition, and content gaps
- **Seasonal Awareness**: Automatic detection of seasonal trends and opportunities
- **Competitor Gap Analysis**: Identification of content opportunities your competitors are missing

#### Strategic Content Planning
- **SEO-Optimized Briefs**: Automatic generation of content briefs with target keywords and search intent analysis
- **Content Calendar Integration**: Intelligent scheduling based on optimal publishing times and frequency
- **Topic Clustering**: Grouping related topics for content series and internal linking strategies
- **Performance Prediction**: AI-powered estimation of content performance potential

### 2. AI-Powered Content Generation

#### Multi-Stage Content Creation
- **Brand-Aware Writing**: Content generation that matches your specific voice, tone, and style guidelines
- **Section-by-Section Generation**: Detailed, structured approach ensuring content quality and flow
- **Research Integration**: Automatic incorporation of current research and data to support claims
- **Internal Linking Optimization**: Smart suggestions for linking to existing content

#### Content Quality Assurance
- **Brand Consistency Validation**: Multi-layer checking to ensure content aligns with brand voice
- **SEO Optimization**: Automatic optimization for target keywords and search intent
- **Readability Analysis**: Content scored and optimized for target audience reading level
- **Fact-Checking Integration**: AI-powered verification of claims and statistics
- **Originality Verification**: Plagiarism detection and uniqueness scoring

#### Content Personalization
- **Audience Targeting**: Content adapted for specific customer segments and personas
- **Industry Expertise**: Deep customization for vertical-specific terminology and concerns
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Content that adheres to industry-specific regulations (HIPAA, GDPR, etc.)
- **Local Optimization**: Geographic and cultural adaptation for different markets

### 3. Visual Content Generation

#### AI-Generated Images
- **DALL-E 3 Integration**: High-quality, contextually relevant hero images for every blog post
- **Brand Style Consistency**: Images that match your brand's visual identity and color palette
- **Multiple Variations**: Generation of 3+ image options for each piece of content
- **Web Optimization**: Automatic image compression and format optimization for fast loading

#### Visual Brand Guidelines
- **Color Palette Integration**: Images generated using your brand's specific color schemes
- **Style Consistency**: Maintained aesthetic across all generated visuals
- **Mood Alignment**: Visual tone that matches content emotion and brand personality
- **Compliance Checks**: Avoidance of potentially problematic visual elements

### 4. Multi-Tenant Platform Architecture

#### Tenant Isolation
- **Complete Data Separation**: Each client's data completely isolated from others
- **Custom Brand Configurations**: Individual voice, style, and strategy settings per tenant
- **Independent Scaling**: Per-tenant resource allocation based on subscription tier
- **Secure API Access**: Tenant-specific API keys and authentication

#### Subscription Tiers
- **Starter Tier**: 10 posts/month, basic customization, standard support
- **Growth Tier**: 50 posts/month, advanced features, A/B testing, priority support
- **Enterprise Tier**: Unlimited posts, white-label options, dedicated support, custom integrations
